About Me

I am a passionate learner! I read, watch tutorials, and even dream about code every day. Leraning new things is what drew me to the coding world. My life in sports made me love working in a team environment. With my previous work histories I have had some of the best customer service training in the world, I like to bring strong customer service to the development world. I am not drawn to any paticular coding language, my belief is that the languages and tools you use should be what is best for the current project, not the tools you are most comfortable in.
